WHY

Data is easy. Decisions are the hard part.

NeaPulse helps editorial teams move from “what happened?” to “what do we do next?”

PROBLEM

Analytics has the numbers, but it rarely tells your team what to change right now. In a busy newsroom, you miss spikes and waste time on the wrong stories.

SOLUTION

NeaPulse highlights what’s gaining momentum, what’s slipping, and what needs attention—so editors can act without digging through charts.

OUTCOME

Faster calls in standups, smarter homepage and social choices, and fewer missed moments when a story is taking off.

What you get (in plain terms)

  • A real-time momentum view that shows what’s rising.
  • A quick “winners vs slipping” snapshot for your next move.
  • Clear comparisons (today vs yesterday) so changes make sense.
  • Rollups by topic, section, or author—so you allocate effort.
  • A newsroom-first layout anyone can read in seconds.

A simple gut-check

If an editor has 30 seconds between meetings, can they answer:

  • What’s taking off right now?
  • What should we promote or refresh today?
  • What’s slipping—and needs a change?

NeaPulse is built to make those answers obvious.
